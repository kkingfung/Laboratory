Weapons are grouped based on **shape** and **material**, making visual recognition and strategic planning more intuitive for players. Each weapon type may be exclusive to a race or shared across multiple races.

## üéØ How Weapon Types Work in Tyrant

- Weapons are drawn each round and determine **attack turns**.
- Each race has access to **2‚Äì3 compatible weapon types**.
- Some weapons are **exclusive to specific races**, while others are **shared**.
- Weapon cards have rarities: `R1`, `R2`, `R3`, affecting their power and deck cost.
